📊 Problem

A company works with prices in USD but needs to analyze them in Argentine pesos (ARS).
The goal is to:
Convert prices from USD to ARS
Show which products are above a certain amount
Count how many products meet that condition

🛒 Dataset
List of products and prices in USD:
Nike Running Shoes — 85 USD
Apple AirPods (2nd Gen) — 129 USD
Logitech Wireless Mouse — 25 USD
Samsung 24 Monitor — 160 USD
Mechanical Keyboard — 75 USD
Backpack Travel — 45 USD
Bluetooth Speaker — 60 USD
External Hard Drive 1TB — 70 USD


💱 Exchange Rate                 🎯 Threshold
Limit: 180000 ARS                1 USD = 1415 ARS 

⚙️ Process

1. Convert the prices of the products list to Ars.

2. Filter and show prices above Threshold.

3. Count the number of those prices.


Conclution: When we convert the prices of the products list to Ars, we can see 2 products above threshold from prices in argentina.
