A company works with prices in USD but needs to analyze them in Argentine pesos.
* You have a list of prices in USD
* Convert them to ARS (you choose the exchange rate)
* Show which ones are above a certain amount
* Count how many meet that condition

    ("Mechanical Keyboard", 75),
    ("Backpack Travel", 45),
    ("Bluetooth Speaker", 60),
    ("External Hard Drive 1TB", 70)
  ]

  price_in_ars = []
  for i in products
